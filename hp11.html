Health Packages

Special & Comprehensive Health Package

1. Advanced Lifestyle Screening

A deep check-up for professionals to assess sugar, cholesterol, liver, kidney, thyroid, vitamins, stress, and heart risk. Helps identify hidden risks early.

Target: Age Group Between 25–45 yrs

Parameters:

  • CBC, HbA1c, Fasting & PPBS
  • Lipid Profile
  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio
  • Liver Function Test
  • Kidney Function Tests (Creatinine, Urea, Uric Acid)
  • Thyroid Profile (TSH, T3, T4, FT3, FT4)
  • Vitamin D, B12, Calcium
  • Hs-CRP, Homocysteine (cardiac risk)
  • Cortisol (stress marker)

2. Fitness & Sports Performance Package

For athletes and fitness lovers — covers blood, sugar, vitamins, electrolytes, hormones, and muscle health. Supports peak performance and recovery.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • Electrolytes (Na, K, Cl, Ca, Mg)
  • HbA1c
  • Fasting Glucose
  • Liver Function Test
  • Kidney Function Tests
  • Creatine Kinase (muscle health)
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin B12
  • Ferritin (energy)
  • Testosterone (men)
  • Estradiol + Progesterone (women)
  • Hormone Profile (Cortisol, DHEAS)

3. Women’s Complete Wellness (Reproductive + Hormonal)

Covers blood, sugar, thyroid, vitamins, calcium, iron, and essential female hormones. A complete health panel for women’s fertility, PCOS, or menopause care.

Target: Women 20–50 yrs, PCOS / fertility / menopause monitoring.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • Thyroid Profile
  • FSH, LH, Prolactin, AMH (fertility hormones)
  • Estradiol, Progesterone
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin B12
  • Calcium
  • Iron Studies
  • HbA1c

Lipid Profile:

  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio

Add-on: Pap Smear / HPV DNA test

4. Men’s Vitality & Prostate Package

Monitors sugar, cholesterol, thyroid, vitamins, testosterone, and prostate health. Tailored for men’s energy, strength, and cancer prevention.

Target: Men 35+ yrs.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • HbA1c

Lipid Profile:

  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio
  • Liver Function Test
  • Kidney Function
  • Thyroid Profile
  • PSA (Prostate)
  • Testosterone
  • DHEAS
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin B12
  • Calcium
  • Homocysteine (cardiac risk)

5. Cancer Risk & Prevention Package

Includes tumor markers, blood tests, vitamins, and sugar/lipid checks. Helps in early detection of cancer risks, especially for those with family history.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • ESR
  • Tumor Markers: PSA (men), CA-125 (women), CEA, AFP
  • Vitamin D, B12, Calcium
  • HbA1c

Lipid Profile:

  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio
  • Homocysteine
  • hs-CRP
  • Pap Smear (women)

6. Bone & Joint Health Package

Covers calcium, vitamin D, parathyroid, arthritis markers, and uric acid. A must for people with bone pain, arthritis, or osteoporosis risk.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • Calcium
  • Phosphorus
  • Vitamin D
  • PTH (Parathyroid Hormone)
  • Alkaline Phosphatase (bone marker)
  • Uric Acid (gout risk)
  • RA Factor
  • Anti-CCP (arthritis screening)
  • HbA1c

Lipid Profile:

  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio

7. Gut Health & Digestion Package

Checks liver, pancreas, stool, H. pylori, vitamins, and iron levels. Designed for patients with acidity, IBS, or digestion problems.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • ESR
  • Liver Function
  • Amylase
  • Lipase
  • Stool Routine & Occult Blood
  • H. pylori Stool Antigen / Urea Breath Test
  • Vitamin B12
  • Folate
  • Iron Studies (Ferritin, TIBC)
  • Food Intolerance 220+ Panel

8. Anti-Aging & Longevity Package

Focuses on oxidative stress, hormones, vitamins, thyroid, sugar, and cardiac risk. A premium package for those who want to age healthier and live longer.

Parameters:

  • CBC
  • HbA1c

Lipid Profile:

  • Total Cholesterol
  • HDL Cholesterol
  • Triglycerides
  • LDL Cholesterol
  • TC/HDL Cholesterol Ratio & LDL/HDL Ratio
  • hs-CRP
  • Homocysteine
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin B12
  • Calcium
  • Iron Studies
  • Hormonal Panel (Testosterone, DHEAS, Cortisol, IGF-1)
  • Thyroid Profile
  • Oxidative Stress Markers (Metabolomix+)
