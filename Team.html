Dr. Rajkumar Nair

Founder of Biosytech Medical Laboratory

A specialist with MRCP (UK) from King’s College, London, and MRCPE, he brings extensive expertise and global training to his practice. A true visionary in healthcare, he pioneered the integration of cutting-edge diagnostics with the finest industry talent, building a Centre of Excellence that redefines standards in the diagnostics space. Today, his leadership continues to shape a trusted partner in healthcare—where innovation meets uncompromising quality.

Mr. Vijay Soni

Chief Executive Officer

With over 20+ years of expertise in Healthcare industry and high-end diagnostics, Mr Vijay has worked with Strong brands in healthcare to drive business growth. He is recognized for his strategic vision, market expansion, and operational excellence in both local and international healthcare markets. A dynamic leader, he continues to position Biosytech at the forefront of innovative and quality-driven diagnostic services.

Dr. Adley Fernandes

MBBS, MD Pathology

Laboratory Director and Consultant Anatomical and Clinical Pathologist Bio: Dr. Adley Mark Fernandes with over 25 years of experience in diagnostics space across India, Saudi Arabia, and the UAE. He has served as Laboratory Director at Biosytech Medical Laboratory, Dubai, since 2007, with expertise in Anatomical Pathology, molecular pathology and quality management. Licensed to practice in multiple regions, he is also a CPHQ, ISO 15189 Internal Auditor, and CAP Inspector. Dr. Fernandes is an active CME/CPD presenter under DHA, committed to advancing diagnostic excellence.

Dr. Vyoma Shah

MBBS, MD Pathology, Specialist Clinical and Hematopathologist

Dr. Vyoma Shah is a Specialist Clinical Pathologist with over 10 years’ experience across India, USA, and the UAE. A postgraduate from Seth G.S. Medical College & KEM Hospital, one of India’s most prestigious medical institutions, she has contributed back to her alma mater by mentoring and tutoring both undergraduate and residency students as an Assistant Professor. A published researcher and ISO 15189-certified internal auditor, she is admired for her leadership, warm personality, and strong interpersonal skills that make her stand out as a valued colleague and a reassuring presence for patients .

Mrs. Janice Javier

Chief Quality Officer

Janice Javier Alcazar has over 20 years of laboratory experience across Philippines and UAE, including a decade in quality management. She specializes in compliance, audits, and laboratory systems oversight. Her expertise spans quality, clinical testing, and laboratory operations, supported by extensive training in ISO 15189, JCI and CAP standards. Recognized for her leadership and dedication, she has regarded as an “Outstanding Emplooyee” in her field.

Mr. Sunjo Cyrilla Berka

Laboratory Supervisor

Sunjo Cyrilla Berka, a graduate of the University of Beau, Cameroon, has been with Biosytech Medical Laboratory for over 10 years. Highly skilled and hardworking, he has in-depth knowledge of laboratory instruments and strong technical expertise in clinical chemistry, virology, serology, and haematology. Mr. Arun Andrew

Head of Operations and Business Alliances

With an MBA in International Business from the UK, he brings expertise in operations management, service delivery, and team leadership. Dr. Aruna Jadhav

MBBS, MD, DNB Microbiology Specialist Medical Microbiologist

Dr. Aruna Jadhav holds a M.D. and D.N.B. in Clinical Microbiology with extensive experience in diagnostic microbiology, molecular techniques across leading laboratories in the UAE and India. Dr. Shaza Ali
