About Biosytech

About Biosytech

Biosytech Medical Laboratory is redefining the standards of diagnostic excellence in the UAE by delivering one of the most comprehensive and advanced pathology services in the region. Recognized as one of the best clinical diagnostic laboratories in the country, Biosytech has become a trusted partner to leading hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions.

Our extensive portfolio of over 4,000+ test codes spans all major specialties, including Haematology, Clinical Chemistry, Immunology, Serology, Medical Microbiology, Histopathology (Anatomical Pathology and Cytology), Molecular Biology, and Genetics.

This breadth of expertise, combined with our cutting-edge technology, allows us to deliver world-class diagnostic insights that drive superior clinical outcomes.

Under the leadership of Dr. Adley Fernandes, and supported by a distinguished team of highly qualified Pathologists, Microbiologists, and Anatomical Pathologists, Biosytech has been serving the healthcare community since 2003. Our unwavering commitment to quality is demonstrated through prestigious international accreditations, including Joint Commission International (JCI, USA) in 2009, followed by College of American Pathologists (CAP) and Emirates International Accreditation Centre (EIAC) in subsequent years.

With state-of-the-art diagnostic infrastructure spanning 10,000+ sq ft and the capability to perform the majority of tests in-house, we ensure rapid turnaround times without compromising accuracy. Operating under the highest international quality standards and stringent internal and external quality controls, Biosytech continues to set benchmarks in laboratory diagnostics across the UAE.

At Biosytech, we have cultivated a quality-driven and innovation-focused culture. Our team is committed to supporting patients, clinicians, clinics, and hospitals with the highest level of professionalism and responsiveness. As a leader in the diagnostics space, we are dedicated to implementing international best practices and setting new benchmarks for laboratory standards across the GCC and Africa.

At Biosytech, our mission is to combine precision, innovation, and service excellence, reinforcing our position as one of the UAE’s leading and most trusted clinical diagnostic laboratories.
